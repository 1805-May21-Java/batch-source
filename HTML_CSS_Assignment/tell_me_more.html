Origin Story

Moderately censored for work appropriateness :)

The "look at this bird" meme originated from Facebook, when hundreds of people posted on the wall of Facebook founder Mack Zuckerberg, all with the previously shown picture of a bird appearing to have caught its head on fire, and the accompanying comment "look at this f***ing bird".

As a result, Mark Zuckerberg disabled the ability to post that comment. For a certain period of time, users were not able to post a comment saying "look at this f***ing bird". It would fail with an error message saying "Failed to post comment" but succeed with any slight misspelling.

The comment has since been enabled, and Facebook continues to profit off selling privacy to advertisers, and the world keeps spinning, but at least we can look at this duck and appreciate that it legitimately made the largest social media platform ban a phrase from its comment section for a couple years.
