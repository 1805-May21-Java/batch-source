We spend so much of our life looking - but never seeing. Maybe there was an old trapper that lived out here and maybe one day he went to check his beaver traps, and maybe he fell into the river and drowned. You don't have to spend all your time thinking about what you're doing, you just let it happen. Fluff it up a little and hypnotize it. Every time you practice, you learn more.

Every single thing in the world has its own personality - and it is up to you to make friends with the little rascals. I can't think of anything more rewarding than being able to express yourself to others through painting. Only God can make a tree - but you can paint one.
