What is pizza?

Pizza is wonderful. Pizza is life. Pizza is love. Life would not be worth living without pizza.

Pizza is typically made from a pizza crust, with a tomato based pizza sauce spread on top and a layer of delicious, delicious cheese on top. Typically the cheese is mozzerlla.
