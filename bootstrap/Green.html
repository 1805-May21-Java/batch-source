Can we learn through Osmosis?


What is osmosis?

Osmosis is the spontaneous net movement of solvent molecules through a selectively permeable membrane into a region of higher solute concentration, in the direction that tends to equalize the solute concentrations on the two sides. Osmosis is a vital process in biological systems, as biological membranes are semipermeable.
