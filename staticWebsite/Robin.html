Robin

About Robin

Robin began his life as Batman's sidekick after the tragin death of his family by the hands of the villain Two-Face. Trained by Batman to one day succeed him in taking his name, Robin employs all the combat and detective skills of his master. While active helping Batman fight crime, he sometimes questions the brutal tactics employed during the fight on crime, preferring a more merciful approach if possible.

Personal Life

Born Dick Grayson, he was a member of the Flying Grayson, a family of circus acrobats. After becoming an orphan, he was subsequently adopted as a ward for Bruce Wayne, maintaining the same cover as his foster father. In some occassions, Dick Grayson has put his personal life at risk to go undercover in situations not possible for Batman.
