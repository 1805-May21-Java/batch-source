Welcome employee

As an employee if this company, this website is meant to help facilitate any and all reimbursement needs. More details can be read below.

Creating Reimbursements

In the "Create Reimbursement" section, you can request a reimbursement by simply writing an amount and submitting the request. A manager will be responsible to determine whether or not the reimbursement will be approved.

Viewing Reimbursements

The "View Reimbursements" section will allow you to view all your reimbursement requests. Once on the page, choose between pending and resolved request to view your Reimbursements. (Note, resolved requests will include requests that have been approved and denied).

Updating Your Information

The "View Information" section will allow you to view your user information. Here you can change your personable info (address, email, phone number). Additionally you can also update your password.
